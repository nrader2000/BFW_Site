Big Fishin World: Ocean Voyager is a fishing roguelike that turns an activity such as ocean fishing and turns it into a dangerous game of luck (reel luck), knowledge, and the ability to think like a fish! Will you be able to make it through a full voyage or will you be thrown OVERBOARD by the cold hearted Cap'N Grant?!

The goal of this game is to complete an ocean fishing voyage around the Island of Phoxinus. In order to do that you must be skilled at knowing the seas of Anglin and how to fish them! Explore various ocean fishing spots and more importantly the fish that reside there! Gain points and progress towards your quota, but will it be enough to get you to the next route? Only the lords of the seas will be able to determine your fate, or will you be able to laugh at the face of them and choose your own destiny on these seas?
